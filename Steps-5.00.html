Key Insight-Positive 5



  • Despite an overall increase in Rent Estimates between September and October 2016, there was a notable 16.2% surge specifically in Single Family Rent Estimate in October 2016. This substantial rise in Rent Estimate indicates a heightened demand for single-family houses during that period.

In March 2017, there was a noteworthy 52.6% decrease in the Violent Crime Rate. This led to a substantial increase in both Rent Estimate and Sales Price, with a rise of 10.7% and an impressive 139.7%, respectively. This surge in demand for houses was a direct result of the significant reduction in the Violent Crime Rate.
