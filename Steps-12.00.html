Creating Calendar Table 12


Steps:


1. Open Power BI Desktop and navigate to the 'Modeling' tab.
2. Click on the 'New Table' button to create a new table.
3. In the DAX formula bar, paste the following formula:
Calendar = ADDCOLUMNS(CALENDAR (Date(Year(Min(Properties_philly_Kraggle_v2[Sale Date])),
Month(Min(Properties_philly_Kraggle_v2[Sale Date])),
DAY(Min(Properties_philly_Kraggle_v2[Sale Date]))),
Date(Year(Max(Properties_philly_Kraggle_v2[Sale Date])),
Month(Max(Properties_philly_Kraggle_v2[Sale Date])),
DAY(Max(Properties_philly_Kraggle_v2[Sale Date])))) ,
"Month number", FORMAT ([Date], "MM"),
"Month Year", FORMAT ([Date], "MMMYYYY"),
"Month", FORMAT ([Date], "mmmm"),
"Week Number", "Week-"& WEEKNUM([Date]),
"Week", WEEKNUM ([Date]),
"Day Of Month", Month([DATE]) &"-"& FORMAT ( [Date], "dd" ),
"Year Quarter", FORMAT ([Date], "YYYY") & "/Q" & FORMAT ([Date], "Q"),
"Year", FORMAT ([Date], "YYYY")
)
4. Press the checkmark icon or hit Enter to validate the formula. And save your Power BI project.
