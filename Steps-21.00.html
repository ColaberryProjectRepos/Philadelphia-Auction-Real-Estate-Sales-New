Adding Map Visual (Sales Price Per Property) 21


Steps:

1. Click on an empty space on the canvas.

2. From the visualizations pane, select ' map'.

3. Configure the Columns and Measures as follows:

· Location: “Zillow Adress”

· Bubble Size: Sales Price

4. Set tooltips to display the following fields: Sale to Bid Ratio, Rent Estimate, Violent Crime Rate, School Score.

5. In the General properties:

· Size: Height(327), Width(328)

· Position: Horizontal(285), Vertical(391)

6. Configure the Title:

· Title 'On'

· Text: 'Sales Price Per Property'

· Font: Segoe UI Bold, 14

· Text Color: #FFFFFF

· Background color: # 426871

· Center alignment

· Text wrap 'On'

· Map Setting: Style: “Road”

· Show Labels on

7. Bubbles:

· Size: -13

· Color: Format: Gradient; Fiel: Violent Crime Rate; Minimum: #EAD187 ; Center: #EDB231; Maximum: #8B6702
