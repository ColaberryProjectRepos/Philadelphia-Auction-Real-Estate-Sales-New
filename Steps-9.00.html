Importing Data to Power BI desktop 9






Project Essentials Zip


Steps:
 1. Get the excel file, download it and save it on your computer by clicking  on the red file button above


2.Go to Power BI Desktop.

3. Click on the "Get Data" button on the home tab in Power BI Desktop.
4. Choose Excel workbook and connect.
5. It will direct you to saved files, choose the ‘Properties-Philly’ excel file and click on Open.
6. Once the data is successfully retrieved, you will see a preview of the data in power BI Desktop, click on Transform Data.
